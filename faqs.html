General Questions

Discover more about us.

What is Landa about?

Landa is an educational technology company that partners with Africa’s top universities and leading global institutions to deliver uninterrupted world-class STEM and Business Education to learners around the world, especially those looking to impact Africa. These educational programs are degrees and certified short courses. Our mission is to accelerate Africa’s development by educating its growth drivers.

How does Landa work?

All programs are delivered entirely online on Landa’s website - www.landalearn.com and can be accessed from anywhere in the world. Landa’s Virtual Learning Campus (VLC) is your virtual classroom for the duration of your program. It is designed to be easy-to-use, learner-friendly and intuitive with an interface that houses and directs you to a variety of course content formats including interactive video lectures, live classes, module notes, group work, quizzes, presentations, assignment briefs, and/or additional web resources.

Learners are required to complete modules, online assignments and other activities that the program requires. Unless stated otherwise on the relevant program/course page or prospectus, you only need a smart device, steady internet connection and enough time to complete your program.

Why do we do what we do?

We are passionate about working at the intersection of higher education and technology, and how this contributes to learner success and Africa’s development. This is at the heart of our mission to accelerate Africa’s development by educating its growth drivers.

Why are we focused on STEM and Business?

There are 2 key reasons. First, STEM and Business have been proven as economic drivers which ties strongly to our mission of accelerating Africa’s development by educating its growth drivers. Second, the worrisome yet increasing rate of unemployment and underemployment in Africa requires us to focus on in-demand skills and industries.

What is STEM?

STEM is an acronym for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics - and it captures the skills and disciplines that can be found in these fields. Some STEM degree programs include computer science, statistics, mathematics, and some engineering courses.

Who are our learners?

Primarily with our degree programs, our learners are young people who want to achieve their tertiary education goals without hassles and interruptions, which can then be used to get fulfilling employment or build their businesses. Secondarily with our short courses, our learners are founders and professionals committed to building successful businesses and careers in Africa.

Is Landa a university?

We are not a university. We partner with only the best accredited universities in Africa and leading institutions, and enable them with technology and support to deliver their quality programs and credentials to millions of learners, including you.

Does Landa offer degrees?

Yes, we offer degree programs from Africa’s top universities in STEM and Business on our platform. Landa also offers premium short courses from top universities in Africa and leading global institutions, and facilitates the issuance of verifiable certificates & applicable credentials from these universities & institutions. We do not issue degrees.

Who issues the degrees and certificates for programs offered on Landa?

Universities and institutions that facilitate the programs that you register for issue the degrees and certificates of those programs. What this means for you is that you get certificates and degrees with just learning and meeting graduation requirements 100% online, without the stress and demand of physical programs.

How do you know that this is legit? That Landa is not a scam?

Our courses and programmes are as legitimate as the universities and institutions that facilitate them. This is not a scam. Every program is facilitated by university faculty who share their vast experience and deep knowledge with learners throughout the program based on accredited curriculums.

In addition to the degrees and certificates awarded by the university upon successful completion of the program, you will also receive a Letter of Validation. You can also contact the university’s representative if you have more concerns.

What makes Landa so affordable?

Landa’s use of technology and the commitment of our partner universities and institutions to opening up the best programs to a high number of qualified students prioritizes our learners, regardless of class and socioeconomic status. We are democratizing opportunities for advancement for all.

Where are we based?

Primarily, our office is headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria with a fully remote team across the world committed to delivering the best education and experience to our learners and partners.

What is in it for you if you refer new learners to Landa?

You will get between 5% - 10% worth of benefits of your referred learner’s payment, when your referral code is confirmed alongside the payment for a program. This applies only when you refer new learners who have not used Landa before.

Admissions
Is there an age restriction?

Yes. Applicants must be 16 years or older at the time of the application deadline.

What documents do you need to apply?

Required documents vary by programs. For all degree programs, you are expected to have your birth certificate or a sworn declaration of age, and a minimum of 5 O-level credits including English Language and Mathematics. Please refer to the program prospectus.

Do you have to take any exam?

Yes. There is a standard entry exam for all programs that you will be required to pass. In some cases, certain universities and programs may require additional checks including interviews. Please refer to the program prospectus.

How do you apply?

Easy. Once you have decided on the program and confirm that you meet the requirements, log on to apply.landalearn.com to start your application. If you have everything ready, it should take you less than 10 minutes to complete the form.

The program prospectus offers you a step-by-step guide to understand what you need to do to complete your application and give yourself the best chance of acceptance.

What is the application fee?

Application fees vary by programs, institutions and citizenship. Application fees are discounted for African students only. They start from $30, over NGN 13,000. Please refer to the program prospectus.

Who are non-African students?

Students that are not citizens of African countries.

Can you start your application and come back later?

Yes, you can pick up where you left off. Make sure you save your application as you fill out the required details.

You missed the application deadline, can you still apply?

Sadly, no. Unless an extension is announced, you will not be able to submit your application, even if you had begun it.

Can you edit your application once it has been submitted?

No, you cannot. We recommend that you carefully review your application and supporting documents before submission.

You have applied to a university before on Landa or elsewhere, can you apply again?

Yes! We encourage you to be consistent in chasing your dreams even if it didn’t work out the last time. We look forward to receiving your application.

Is there an admission option for Direct Entry students to begin from the 2nd year?

No, we currently do not have a Direct Entry option. We are working on ensuring that this option is available and will announce updates in due course.

Can current full-time students transfer to Landa’s online program?

Currently, no. We will continue to explore options to make learning easier for all students and will make announcements when there are updates.

How do you know if your application is successful or not?

We will notify you via email and your Landa profile. We wish you the very best!

Can you defer your admission?

Yes, you can defer your admission by a year. Terms may vary by programs and institutions. Please refer to the program prospectus.

Graduation
What are the clearance requirements for graduation?

To be eligible for graduation, you'll need to have met all the requirements of your degree. That means you've completed all your classes, assignments, and final project within the required timeframe. It also means that all your admission documents have been cleared and you are not owing fees. Please refer to the program prospectus.

Are you able to attend a convocation event to celebrate your graduation?

Depending on the program and the university, you may be eligible for an online convocation ceremony. Some programs may offer physical options for graduation ceremonies while some do not offer convocation ceremony options. Please refer to the program prospectus.

Do you get a degree and transcript after you complete all requirements?

Yes. Your degree and transcript will be available from the 3rd week of graduating. Your degree certificate (physical and high-quality electronic version) will be available for $75. The electronic version will be uploaded on your Landa account, while the physical copy will be delivered to your postal address on record.

A student copy of your transcript will be uploaded on your Landa account for a one-time fee of $25 which you can print as many times as you want.

Are they the same degree certificates that full-time students get?

Yes they are, except a program’s prospectus states otherwise. It will not mention that you studied online, but you should be proud of your online qualification. Online education has seen tremendous growth over the past decade.

Are these degrees internationally recognized?

Yes. The degrees are issued by leading, internationally recognized universities, in collaboration with Landa. Awarded degrees on Landa are as recognized and credible as the issuing universities or institutions.

Can graduates from university programs on Landa serve with the NYSC?

No. However, you are eligible for a Letter of Exclusion that the NYSC issues to candidates who are graduates but could not attend regular full-time programs in school. When there is a different regulation from the NYSC on this, we will ensure to let you know.

What next after graduation?

Join the Alumni Association of your graduating university. Please refer to your program’s prospectus. Then, fulfil your post-varsity dreams! Apply for that job or post-graduate program, or start your business. You have the freedom to become anything!
